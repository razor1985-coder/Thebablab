📋 Rules — v20.8 (R-WC74 to R-WC125 + Knockout Learning Rules)
v15 GATE RULES:
• BAB must show VALUE (positive EV) to place
• Combined odds must be ≥ 3.0 — below this, not enough return for multi-leg risk
• Maximum 2 BABs per session — quality over volume
• If nothing clears both gates → NO BETS for this game
• EV % is directional only — model in calibration, WC sample sizes are small
R-WC74 Card rate doubles at fc≥2 (16% vs 6%). Always anchor card leg on 2+ fc history player.
R-WC75 Tackles market: D/M hit 2+ at 40-43%. Forwards only 15%. Never use F for tackles leg.
R-WC76 SOT market: Forwards 52%, Midfielders 27%, Defenders 13%. Weight SOT to forwards only.
R-WC77 Blowout kills foul markets. In 4+ goal defeats, losing team stops fouling. Strip foul/card legs vs elite opponents likely to win big.
R-WC78 Shots created (kp) market is a MID market first. Midfielders 29%, Forwards 20%, Defenders 14%.
R-WC79 Attacking fullbacks = elite fouls-won assets. Any FB with 3+fd in either game = confirmed asset.
R-WC80 Han-Beom Lee + Krejčí confirmed ELITE: 3fc in BOTH games. Highest confidence fc assets in database.
R-WC81 Modiba + Mudau (RSA): 3+ tackles BOTH games. Nikola Katić (BOS): 5+3 tackles confirmed.
R-WC82 Match importance multiplier: both-must-win = +25% foul/card confidence. Elimination = +35%.
R-WC83 Captain = 90 mins. Never apply sub risk to confirmed captain unless injury confirmed pre-match.
R-WC84 Luxury player warning. Strip from foul/SOT legs vs strong defensive opponents. They disappear in tight games.
R-WC85 GS3 card escalation: group stage game 3 produces more cards. Upgrade card confidence in all GS3 games.
R-WC86 HT trailing effect: aggressive subs at 60 mins when chasing. Flag likely impact subs for card/foul legs.
R-WC87 Surface flag: artificial turf = faster game, more physical. Upgrade foul market confidence.
R-WC88 (CRITICAL) Strip fouls committed leg for any player on winning team when scoreline reaches 2-0 before 60 mins. Pressing drops, fouls dry up. Adams 1fc in 2-0 USA vs AUS confirms this pattern. Never rely on fouls committed in comfortable wins.
R-WC89 Tantashev WC_PASSIVE confirmed — 2 YC only in SCO vs MAR despite 3.5 career avg. R-WC53 validated again. Strip ALL card legs when Tantashev officiates.
R-WC90 Lewis Ferguson ELITE fd+tackles dual asset — 3fd + 7 tackles vs Morocco. Both markets viable every Scotland game.
R-WC91 Juan Cáceres (PAR) ELITE tackles — 9 tackles vs Turkey, highest in database. Back 2+ tackles every Paraguay game.
R-WC92 Elite dribblers in wide/AM roles vs high-press opponents = extreme fd counts. Güler 6fd vs PAR confirms R-WC79 extension.
R-WC93 GS3 suspension filter — Almirón RED vs Turkey, auto-strip from PAR GS3 BABs. Always check reds from previous game.
R-WC94 Blowout card clustering — in 3+ goal margin games, cards cluster on the LOSING/CHASING side not the dominant team. In NED 5-1 SWE, all 3 Oliver YC went to Sweden. Never back dominant team players for cards in expected blowouts. Shift card picks to underdog/chasing side.
R-WC95 SOT vs Tackles reclassification — Nmecha 8 tackles/0 SOT, Sangare 7 tackles/0 card vs GER confirms: high-volume tackle players suppress their own SOT and card output. Reclassify any player showing 5+ tackles in DB to tackles market only. Strip SOT and card legs.
R-WC96 Sub-before-card pattern — Sangare and Pavlovic both subbed off before receiving cards in GER vs CIV despite HIGH ref. Card legs on players who were subbed 60-75' in their previous game carry elevated sub risk. Check starting role confirmation pre-match.
R-WC97 Poisson EV gate — for SOT/fouls markets, Poisson lambda calc available in EV calculator. Use lambda = player avg per game. P(X≥1) = 1 - e^(-λ). Compare to implied probability from odds. If Poisson prob > implied prob = positive EV.
R-WC98 YC accumulation flag — players with 1 YC this tournament face elevated next-card risk in physical/high-card-ref games. Flag in database. In GS3 (R-WC85) this is especially relevant — players protecting a yellow may paradoxically foul more to compensate.
R-WC99 Leg result logging — every BAB leg must now log individual outcome: ✅ Won / ❌ Lost / 🔄 Super Sub fired / ⚠️ Void. Include reason for loss. This enables pattern detection on which leg types fail most frequently.
R-WC100 ⚠️ CRITICAL Always wait for CONFIRMED official lineups before building BABs — never use predicted lineups. Confirmed 21 Jun S20 ESP vs KSA: Gavi/Torres/Fabián all benched despite predicted lineup — both original BABs dead before KO. If confirmed lineup not available, DO NOT PLACE.
R-WC101 ⚠️ CRITICAL Blowout kills BOTH fouls committed AND fouls won (extends R-WC77). When a team is winning 3+ goals, the opposition stops attacking — fouls WON market dries up. KSA 0-4 down: Moteb 0fd despite elite profile. Strip BOTH fc AND fd legs for any player on a team likely to lose 3+ goals.
R-WC102 Any player from a weaker nation showing 0 WC matches = UNKNOWN ASSET. Sub risk is HIGH by default. Never use fouls committed or involvement legs without checking sub history. S23: Ismael (IRQ) 0 WC data → subbed → 0fc.
R-WC103 Fouls WON on pacey attackers vs deep blocks is the highest confidence foul market. Prioritise over fouls committed when both available — fouls won players don't get subbed tactically and output doesn't change with scoreline (unless blowout).
R-WC104 Tackles market is systematically underpriced at WC. Players hitting 3+ tackles in GS1 are confirmed assets. Market pays 1.73–2.00 for 3+ consistently. Mac Allister 4, Doski 4, Bensebaini 6, Abu Taha 7, Koné 3, Mané 3 — all confirmed.
R-WC105 Ref foul discount tiers: WC_PASSIVE (Omar, Tantashev, Mariani) = 20% discount to career foul avg. MOD_HIGH (Fischer, Turpin) = 10% discount. HIGH (Herrera, Tejera, Pinheiro, Zwayer) = 5% or none. S22: Laimer 0fc vs 3.0 avg under Omar — severe suppression confirmed.
R-WC106 Banker SOT legs (Messi/Mbappé/Haaland) are near-certain but too short to anchor a BAB (1.04–1.17). Use as booster legs to push combined odds up only. All three hit every session tracked.
R-WC107 Omar ref profile UPDATED: LOW FOULS confirmed but NOT zero cards. Showed 4 YC in ARG 2-0 AUT. Do NOT strip cards entirely — reduce confidence one tier only. Primary strip is fouls committed legs.
R-WC108 Shots created market is underpriced vs SOT. KEY ADVANTAGE: Super Sub protection — sub risk rules R-WC54/59 do NOT apply. Best candidates: attacking fullbacks, creative CMs, wide forwards. AVOID when player's team expected to sit deep — shots created collapses in defensive setups.
R-WC109 ⚠️ CRITICAL — CARDS ARE ANTI-PREDICTIVE Database analysis of 612 two-game players: a player carded in GS1 repeated in GS2 only 5% of the time vs an 8% base rate — predictive lift x0.62. Cards do NOT carry game to game. NEVER anchor or kingpin a BAB on a card leg. Card legs are supplementary value ONLY (BAB5 longshot / free-bet tier). This explains S24 (Fayzullaev card) and S25 (Partey card) losses — both anchored on the single least reliable market available.
R-WC110 — REPEATABILITY HIERARCHY (anchor priority) Predictive lift (repeat rate ÷ base rate) from full DB: Shots created/key passes 2+ = x2.25 (BEST). Tackles 3+ = x1.93. Fouls won 2+ = x1.78. SOT 1+ = x1.67. Tackles 2+ = x1.50. Cards = x0.62 (WORST). Build anchors from the TOP of this list. Promote tackles and shots-created to primary anchor candidates — they are the most repeatable markets in the dataset and we have been underusing them.
R-WC111 — REPEAT-CONFIRMED ANCHOR POOL A player who hit a threshold in BOTH group games is materially more likely to hit a third time. Prioritise these for GS3 anchors. Tackles: Cáceres (PAR), Nmecha (GER), Lasheen (EGY), Aït-Nouri (ALG), El Aynaoui (MAR), Hincapié (ECU), Guimarães (BRA). Fouls won: Chong (CUR), Mejbri (TUN), Ferguson (SCO), Güler (TUR), Hakimi (MAR), N.Mendes (POR), Baturina (CRO). SOT: Messi, Mbappé, Haaland, J.David, Gakpo, Gyökeres.
R-WC112 — LOW-SCORING GAME CARD SUPPRESSION A ref can only card what is in front of him. When combined xG projection is under 2.0 and/or both teams set up to contain, demote ALL card legs to LOW regardless of referee tier. S25 ENG 0-0 GHA: MOD_HIGH ref Martínez but cagey game produced minimal cards, both card legs died. Tight low-intensity games suppress cards the same way defensive finals do.
R-WC113 — GS3 DEAD-RUBBER FILTER In GS3, check each team's qualification scenario BEFORE building. Already-qualified or already-eliminated teams rotate heavily and play at lower intensity — strip involvement and foul legs, expect heavy rotation (apply R-WC54/59 sub risk aggressively). MUST-WIN / both-must-win games = high intensity, upgrade foul + tackle confidence (stacks with R-WC82). This filter matters more in GS3 than any prior round.
R-WC114 — KNOCKOUT CORNERS Group stage ban (R-WC15) lifted for knockout rounds only. Team must avg 5.5+ corners/game. Combined market needs both teams 5+. Never use in group games.
R-WC115 — KNOCKOUT MOTIVATION No dead rubbers in knockout stage. Strip GS3 dead-rubber suppressor. ALL players assumed full motivation unless injured or suspended. This INCREASES confidence in foul/involvement legs vs group stage.
R-WC116 — KNOCKOUT EXTRA TIME RISK Games can go to 120 mins + pens. Players with sub risk in group stage may play deeper into games in knockout. Extend confirmed minutes threshold by 15 mins. If player was subbed at 70 in GS, treat as 85 min player in knockout for sub-risk assessment.
R-WC117 — KNOCKOUT CAGEY GAME SUPPRESSOR QF and SF games are more conservative than R16. Top-8 nations vs top-8 nations: flag as potential cagey context. Suppress SOT/shots-created legs, elevate tackles/defensive involvement. R16 is most similar to group stage — apply full confidence. QF+: apply 15% SOT reduction.
R-WC118 — KNOCKOUT OPPONENT QUALITY When elite vs elite (top-8 nations): suppress SOT for attackers whose GS numbers came vs weak opposition. Mbappé vs minnows ≠ Mbappé vs Brazil. Apply quality downgrade multiplier on any asset whose 3-game avg is skewed by a weak GS1 opponent.
R-WC119 — KNOCKOUT fc/fd HIERARCHY SHIFT Historic WC knockout data: fouls won hold more stable than fouls committed in knockouts (fd -0%, fc -15%). Promote fd legs above fc legs for knockout BABs. Tackles INCREASE +10% in knockouts (defensive urgency). Cards: R16 only as supplementary — strip from QF onwards unless HIGH ref active confirmed.
R-WC120 — KNOCKOUT SOT DECAY SOT drops ~20% in QF/SF vs R16. R16 = full confidence on SOT bankers. QF = apply 15% downgrade to SOT legs. SF = 20% downgrade. Only KNOCKOUT TIER 1 bankers (Messi 4.0avg, Haaland 3.5avg, Mbappé 3.0avg) retain full SOT confidence in QF/SF.
R-WC121 — FLANK MATCHUP (CRITICAL) Before ANY fullback tackles leg, confirm which opposition wide player lines up against them. If the dangerous wide attacker is on the OPPOSITE flank, downgrade or strip the tackles leg. Modiba 0 tackles vs Canada confirmed: Buchanan played RM attacking Mudau's side, not Modiba's LB side. Formation + flank context always overrides raw average. This check is MANDATORY pre-placement.
R-WC122 — SUPER SUB PROFILE CHECK Super Sub protection only works if the likely replacement has a compatible statistical profile. Before placing any fd/fc leg, check the bench. If the probable sub is a different player type (dribbler not fouled, defensive not creative), flag or strip the leg. Alphonso Davies replaced Buchanan: Davies=dribbler, 0fd — completely incompatible with fd market. Never assume the Super Sub mechanic transfers to an incompatible replacement profile.
R-WC123 — POSSESSION-DOMINANT OPPONENT SUPPRESSES fd When the opposition dominates possession patiently (62%+ avg), the defensive team's DMs are bypassed via passing not dribbling — their fouls-won rate drops sharply. Mokoena 0fd vs Canada (62% possession) confirmed. Only back DM fd legs when the opponent is expected to press high, play direct, or attack at pace — not when they are a patient possession team.
R-WC124 — WC PERFORMANCE OVERRIDES CAREER AVG (REFS) Referee career averages do not reliably predict WC knockout performance. Pinheiro career HIGH (4.8 YC/game) but produced only 3YC+1RC vs SUI/BIH and 2YC vs RSA/CAN in WC context. Always weight confirmed WC match data above career stats. Maintain WC-specific YC tracking for all refs who appear in multiple knockout games.
R-WC125 — STRIKER SOT ROLE CHECK (KO) In knockout games where the favourite dominates possession vs a deep block, the primary striker may drop to link play rather than attack the box — suppressing their SOT. Check if team plays two forwards: the second striker (more physical, direct) may generate equal or higher SOT than the name striker. Canada vs RSA: Oluwaseyi 2 SOT, David 1 SOT. Always check both striker profiles before anchoring SOT on name alone.
